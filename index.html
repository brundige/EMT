Built on Open Hardware Architecture

Leveraging proven open-source platforms to deliver enterprise-grade V2X capabilities at accessible prices.

Core Features

  • Dual-core ESP32 fully programmable SoC
  • High performance Wi-Fi and Bluetooth communications
  • G-force measurement and motion/orientation detection
  • High accuracy and high update rate GNSS geolocation
  • Access to all standard OBD-II PIDs, DTC, VIN from vehicle ECU
  • CAN bus data sniffing
  • Car battery voltage reading
  • Data storage in internal Flash or microSD card (up to 32GB)
  • Real-time data transmission over WiFi and 4G cellular network

Technical Specifications

  • Espressif ESP32 with 16MB Flash
  • Built-in 802.11 b/g/n Wi-Fi and dual mode Bluetooth (classic and BLE)
  • Integrated ICM-42627 motion sensor
  • Integrated u-blox M10 GNSS module and antenna
  • Integrated SIM7600A-H LTE CAT-4 or SIM7070G global LTE CAT-M cellular module
  • Integrated microSD card reader
  • Integrated buzzer
  • Compact enclosure: 60x48x20mm
